About Me

Lago Brian

I'm a Financial Engineer and Data Scientist based in Nairobi, Kenya, working at the intersection of quantitative finance, machine learning, and algorithmic trading. With a background in both academic finance and real-world market application, I build systems that translate complex financial theory into production-ready tools.

Before finance, I served in the military — an experience that instilled in me a deep appreciation for structured thinking, risk management under pressure, and disciplined execution. These traits translate directly to quantitative research: the discipline to follow a process even when markets are noisy, and the conviction to act when the signal is clear.
